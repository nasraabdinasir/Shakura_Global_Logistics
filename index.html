Working Process

Our Logistics Process From Order to Delivery

At Shakura Global Express, our operations are streamlined to ensure smooth, dependable, and customized logistics.
From the initial request to final delivery, every step is planned for speed, accuracy, and reliability.

Step 1: Order & Requirements Capture

Shipment begins when the customer places
an order and Shakura staff collect key
information about the package, destination, and client preferences.

Step 2: Real-Time Tracking Initialization

The package is tagged using GPS and RFID systems.
These enable constant visibility from the point of dispatch, with updates automatically logged at each checkpoint.

Step 3: Smart Processing & Routing

Shipment data is processed through our cloud-powered system to calculate the most efficient route. This minimizes delays and adapts dynamically to traffic or customs bottlenecks.

Step 4: Secure Delivery & Notifications

Packages are delivered securely to their final destination. Customers receive real-time updates and confirmations through the online portal or SMS/email alerts.
